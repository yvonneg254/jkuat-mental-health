FAQ / Support

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What services does the mental health platform offer?

The platform provides resources, counseling services, and support for students and staff.

2. How can I access counseling services?

Counseling services can be accessed through our appointment scheduling page on the platform.

3. Is my information kept confidential?

Yes, all information is kept confidential and used only for the purposes of providing support.

4. What should I do in a mental health emergency?

If you are in immediate danger or require urgent help, please contact emergency services or visit the nearest health facility.
